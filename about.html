About Us

Hello! The Heatmap Monitoring and Alert System was built for the SmartSync senior design group at UNT. I built this monitoring and alert system to try to create an easy method of tracking and alerting guests of potential risk of contamination from someone who has a high fever. This system also allows for keeping track of high traffic areas and allows users to search database history by room, date, and location. The flags are manually reset using a hardware push button, therefore if you do not see a flag, the room is safe to enter! Thank you for using our website!
